What should i start with?Despite the multiplicity of learning sources and the availability of many courses that allow you to learn anything you want, this multiplicity is not very useful because it often causes distraction for the learner and makes them delay in determining where to start and how much information they need to store in their field.

Therefore, this department was designed, which contains specific paths that you follow to learn the skills required in the market, such as Digital Marketing, Graphic Design, Programming, Public Relations, and Human Resources.

While determining the paths, specific specifications of the sources were taken into account:

  1. It should include beginner level and above.
  2. The sources must be free.
  3. It must be in Arabic or English
 Important Note: This path will get you started learning the field through reliable sources, but in order to achieve the greatest benefit and progress, you must:
  1. Follow famous people in this field on social media and take their advice.
  2. Try to apply the theoretical part that you have learned. This can be done by applying for a job or training in a company or volunteer work close to your field.

Public Relations

Public relations is the practice of managing the spread of information between an individual or an organization and the public. Public relations may include an organization or individual gaining exposure to their audiences using topics of public interest and news items that do not require direct payment.

Human Resources

Human resources is the set of people who make up the workforce of an organization, business sector, industry, or economy. A narrower concept is human capital, the knowledge which the individuals embody.
